'Go tapa' is the Irish phrase for 'quickly' or 'rapidly'. Our aim as an ethical, environmentally aware app is to enable users to plan their Dublin Bus journey fast and hassle free. Sign up to activate your free user account and start logging carbon bus points. Log your journey and earn bus points which can be redeemed for discounted journeys and prizes. With every milestone achieved using Dublin Bus you are actively helping the environment and reducing the amount of carbon emissions being released in Dublin.
